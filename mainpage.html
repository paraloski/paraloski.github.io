about basketball for players

basketball for players is a website with alot of tips for basketball players that want new moves added to their bag

in basketball for players we have 3 categories. post, offensive and defensive.

at the categorie post we specificly talk about moves that u can use on a area on the court where a player with the ball can set up for a shot or pass.

at the categorie offensive we talk about important dribbeling moves to get past a defender when u have the ball

and at the categorie defense we of course talk about defensive moves, strategies and tips.
